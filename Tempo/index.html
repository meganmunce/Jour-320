About

Learn More About Me

I'm currently a junior at Northwestern University studying journalism and political science with an interest in local politics, data visualization and interactive journalism.

Through a combination of classes at Northwestern and self-teaching, I've gained skills for both traditional print journalism and digital media.

  • Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, AfterEffects, Audition)
  • Proficient in data visualization (Tableau, R, Python)
  • Professional fluency in Spanish

As a Silicon Valley native, I've grown up learning the ways technology can make information more accessible. It's my passion to do the same with journalism. Through using interactive elements, multimedia storytelling and digital visualizations, I hope to create journalism that is accessible, informative and engaging.
